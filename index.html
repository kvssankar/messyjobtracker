Key Features

Seamless Gmail Integration

Connects directly to your Gmail to automatically identify and track job application-related emails, turning chaos into clarity.

Smart Application Tracking

Organizes job emails by status (applied, interview, offer, rejected) based on smart analysis, giving you a clear overview of your pipeline.

Domain Exclusion

Easily exclude specific domains (e.g., newsletters, spam) from being tracked, ensuring your focus remains on genuine job applications.

Your Data, Your Browser

All your application data is stored locally within your Chrome browser, ensuring your privacy and control over your sensitive information.

Intuitive Dashboard

Get a quick glance at your application statistics, upcoming follow-ups, and overall progress with a clean and simple interface.

Lightweight & Fast

Designed to be efficient and non-intrusive, the extension works silently in the background without slowing down your Browse experience.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

We believe in full transparency. The Messy Job Tracker processes your data locally and never sends your sensitive information to external servers. Learn more about how we protect your data.
